## ðŸ’³ How to Make a Sale (Point of Sale)

This is how you sell something to a customer in the shop:

### Step 1: Find the Product

**Option A - Type the name:**
- Type the product name in the search box
- Click on the product when you see it

**Option B - Scan barcode:**
- Click the camera icon ðŸ“·
- Point your camera at the barcode
- The product appears automatically

### Step 2: Check the Cart

- See what's in the cart on the right side
- Use **+** and **-** to change quantity
- Click **X** to remove something

### Step 3: Choose How Customer Pays

Click one of these:
- **Cash** - They pay with money
- **Mobile Money** - They pay with their phone
- **Card** - They pay with bank card
- **Bank Transfer** - They send to your bank
- **Credit** - They will pay later

### Step 4: Finish the Sale

1. If cash, type how much money they gave you
2. Click **"Complete Sale"**
3. A receipt appears
4. Click **"Print"** to give them a receipt

**Done! The sale is saved and stock is updated automatically.**

## ðŸ“¦ How to Add a New Product

1. Click **"Products"** in the menu
2. Click **"Add Product"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name** - What is the product called?
   - **Category** - What type of product? (e.g., Cables, Routers)
   - **Supplier** - Who do you buy it from? *(Required!)*

   - **Stock** - How many do you have?

5. Click **"Save"**

**Important:** You MUST choose a supplier. No supplier = cannot save.

## ðŸ†˜ Common Problems

### "I can't save the product"
- Make sure you selected a **Supplier**. Every product needs a supplier.

### "Product not showing on website"
- Check if the product is **Active** (is_active = Yes)
- Check if it has **Stock** (stock_quantity > 0)

### "Barcode scanner not working"
- Click **Allow** when browser asks for camera
- Make sure there's good lighting
- Hold the barcode steady

### "Receipt not printing"
- Check your printer is connected
- Try "Print" button again
- Use browser print (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P)

### "Wrong date showing"
- The system uses **Uganda time (EAT)**
- All dates are in local Kampala time
